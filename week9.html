Link's Lawn Service

Making yards across Hyrule look great since 1986.

When he's not saving Hyrule, the Hero of Time services lawns. With a blade as sharp as the Master Sword, he can cut your grass as high or low as you'd like. And as a plus, if he finds any rupees, your cut is free! He'll also gladly clear out any other random debris such as arrows, bombs, deku seeds, etc.
